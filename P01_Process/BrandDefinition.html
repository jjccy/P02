DING Hotel is notable for its design. It is focused on the visual concept, on its architecture, on interior decoration, on aesthetics style and décor. The appearance is the main appeal of a H Hotel and the aim is to evoce a ‘wow’ feeling in the (potential) guest.

DING Hotel is designed by a famous architect. As an expression of his unique style it gives the message ‘one-of-a-kind’.

DING Hotel is a modern, contemporary and cutting-edge, minimalistic and angular. Both exterior and interior design is high style.
