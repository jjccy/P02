MISSION PURPOSE VALUES

MISSION



Inspired by the sea, The DING is an exceptional new destination on Hainan Island, just off the coast of Southern China. Water, a source of vitality and serenity, defines the lush 50-acre DING property, which features a spectacular private ocean along with two pools.

All of the 506 guest rooms in the horseshoe-shaped hotel have ocean views. In addition, 11 hotel villas are nestled into a terraced hillside with ocean views, private pools, and pavilion spaces.

Nearby attractions abound, such as shopping at luxury designer outlets, but once guests cross the entrance into The DING, they become part of an experience that is other-worldly.

PURPOSE



Spanning over 2000 square meters on two levels with extensive lush gardens and a seamless connection to nature, The Spa at The DING reflects a sophisticated approach to beauty, wellness, and wellbeing equally attuned to modern living and local customs.

VALUES



All of the 506 guest rooms in the horseshoe-shaped hotel have ocean views. The warm and inviting interiors exude a comfortable, simple sophistication, using light oak finishes and airy fabrics that evoke the beach. Continued with lime stone floors from Indonesia and a clean, light color palate. Bathtubs are positioned in the main room, surrounded by views, the space becomes even more luxurious and open.
