ROOMS

Inspired by the sea, The Sanya DING is an exceptional new destination on Hainan Island, just off the coast of Southern China. Water, a source of vitality and serenity, defines the lush 50-acre DING property, which features a spectacular private ocean along with two pools.

All of the 506 guest rooms in the horseshoe-shaped hotel have ocean views. In addition, 11 hotel villas are nestled into a terraced hillside with ocean views, private pools, and pavilion spaces.

Nearby attractions abound, such as shopping at luxury designer outlets, but once guests cross the entrance into The Sanya DING, they become part of an experience that is other-worldly.
